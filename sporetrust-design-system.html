Motion

Two easing curves and four durations. Interactions are quick and confident (160ms); reveals are slow and observed (900ms). Hover lifts are a 1–2px translate, never a bounce.

/* easing */
--ease-out:    cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1);
--ease-in-out: cubic-bezier(0.65, 0, 0.35, 1);

/* duration */
--dur-fast:   160ms;   /* hover, taps */
--dur-base:   260ms;   /* card transitions */
--dur-slow:   520ms;   /* section shifts */
--dur-reveal: 900ms;   /* scroll reveals, mould growth */

Eyebrows, tags & badges

Small mono labels do the categorising. Eyebrows in green sit above headings; tags mark step/method numbers and severity; the solid green badge flags the featured pricing tier.

how we diagnose Thermal Severe Moderate Low risk Most booked

Severity colour map: high = ink, moderate = ink-soft, low = green. Never use red/amber/green stoplight colours — severity reads through weight and the surrounding data, in keeping with the neutral, evidence-first tone.

Patterns

Forms & inputs

The suburb-check pattern is the front door to the booking flow. Inputs sit on paper with a line border; focus rings are green. The leading mono label keeps the instrument-panel tone.

Inputs use min-width:0 inside flex rows so long placeholder text never forces horizontal overflow — a common container break this system guards against.

Patterns

FAQ / disclosure

Native <details> rows, line-divided, max 760px wide. A mono +/– marker in green signals state. Used for "Before you book" and route-page question sets.

Are you a remediation company?

No. We inspect, document and report. If remediation or building work is needed, we can point you toward vetted providers — but we don't clip the ticket on the cleanup.

How long does the on-site assessment take?

Most homes take around 45 minutes. Larger homes or sites with complex history can take longer; we'll let you know in advance.

When do I get the report?

Rapid Inspection reports land in your portal within 48 hours. Lab-Backed reports include an analysis appendix 5–7 days after sampling.

Convention · load-bearing

Section background tiers

The background colour communicates a section's intent before any copy is read. Three tiers, applied consistently. When adding a section, reuse a tier — don't introduce a one-off colour.

Cream — Awareness

Problem awareness, education, framing. The mould-effect canvas attaches here by intent.

.problem-bg · .signs-section · .faq

White — Solution

Solution education, service communication, methodology.

.solution · .route-page-banner · .faq--white

Ink — Intent to act

Direct CTA, product surfaces, "intent to proceed" moments where the goal is action.

.hero · .sentinel-card · .trust-bar
Why it matters: this convention is load-bearing across both the marketing site and the /admin tool. The reader should sense "am I learning or am I acting?" from the surface alone.
